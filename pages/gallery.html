If you see something cool, take a screenshot and email it to me! I'll add it to the gallery. To take a screenshot, first press f1 to clear your cursor crosshairs and arm from the screen, then press f2 to take the screenshot. Press f1 again to get your cursor back. Screenshots are stored in the .minecraft folder inside the FTB folder.
